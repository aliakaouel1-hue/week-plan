Your Focus Toolkit

⚡ For procrastination

  • 2-minute rule: if you're stuck, just open the file. That's the only goal.
  • Pomodoro timer: 25 min work, 5 min break. Use pomofocus.io.
  • Body doubling: Focusmate session or lo-fi music on YouTube.
  • Name the resistance: write "I'm avoiding this because __" before you start.
  • No perfect start: open a blank artboard and place one shape. That's enough.

📵 For social media

  • Cold Turkey / Freedom app: hard block Instagram, TikTok, Twitter 9am–6pm.
  • iOS Screen Time: set a 30-min/day limit with a passcode someone else sets.
  • Grayscale mode: turn your phone to grayscale during work hours.
  • Allowed times only: 1:00–1:30pm and 7:00–7:30pm. Timer on.
  • Replace the habit: open Notion or your moodboard instead.
